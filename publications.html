Publications

The publication is one form (perhaps the most internationally accessible form) of presenting research outcomes. We believe top-class publications are essentially from top-class research works. At SODA, our goal is to identify interesting research problems with engineering and/or theoretical significance and develop practical and innovative methodologies to solve them. After this tough but enjoyable process, our research outcomes are published as the papers below.
