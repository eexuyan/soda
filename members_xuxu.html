Brief Biography:

Dr. Xu Xu received the BEng Degree in School of Electrical Engineering from Qingdao University of Technology, China in 2015, and the MSc and PhD degree from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong in 2016 and 2019, respectively. He ever served as the researcher in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong and The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong. Currently, He is a Research Fellow in School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interests include power asset health modeling and monitoring, power system optimization and application of artificial intelligence in power system.
