Email:

Research Topic: Power Asset Health Modeling and Monitoring, Risk-based Power Asset Investment and Planning Optimization, Power Equipment Insulation Online Monitoring and Fault Diagnosis of , Situational Awareness in Power Distribution System

Brief Biography:

Nan Zhou received the B.E. degree and Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering respectively in 2016 and 2021, both from the Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. He is currently working as a research fellow at SP Group-NTU Joint Lab, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interests include power asset health modeling and monitoring, power equipment insulation online monitoring and fault diagnosis, and situational awareness in power distribution system.
